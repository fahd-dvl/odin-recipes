lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made of layered pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. The base typically consists of wide, flat pasta sheets stacked with rich fillings like ground beef or pork, marinara sauce, ricotta or b√©chamel cheese, and mozzarella. It is then baked in the oven until golden and bubbly, creating a hearty, savory meal. Lasagna can be customized with various ingredients, such as vegetables or different types of cheese, making it a versatile and beloved comfort food worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Meat
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Egg
  • Herbs & Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles (if not oven-ready)
  4. Preheat the Oven
  5. Assemble the Lasagna
  6. Bake the Lasagna
  7. Rest and Serve
