pizza

A pizza is a classic Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough that is typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients such as vegetables, meats, or seafood. The base is usually baked in an oven until the crust becomes crispy and golden brown

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella)

Steps

  • Preheat the Oven
  • Prepare the Dough
  • Add the Sauce
  • Add the Cheese
  • Add Optional Toppings (if desired)
  • Bake
  • Rest and Serve
